# Singer Website Research

This project started as inspiration research for premium singer websites and was
then adapted into a factual, image-led site for **Preethi Yagnamurthy**.

## Artist-site inspiration

1. Dua Lipa official site
   https://www.dualipa.com/
   Why it stands out: campaign-first homepage, giant headline, and very limited
   actions focused on listening, signup, and adjacent brand content.

2. Dua Lipa agency case study
   https://republika.tv/project/dualipa-com/
   Why it stands out: the agency describes the official site as an immersive
   fan experience that connects music, tour, and exclusive content.

3. Charli xcx official site
   https://www.charlixcx.com/
   Why it stands out: era-specific art direction, asymmetrical UI block, and
   strong creative credits in the footer so the site feels authored.

4. Lorde official site
   https://www.lorde.co.nz/
   Why it stands out: radical restraint. The homepage is essentially an album
   portal, which proves that a singer site can work with very little chrome.

5. Tinashe official site
   https://www.tinashenow.com/home
   Why it stands out: one-page structure that combines tour, video, album, bio,
   and contact without losing clarity.

6. FKA twigs - EUSEXUA
   https://eusexua.fkatwi.gs/
   Why it stands out: immersive fullscreen world-building with edge-positioned
   controls, strong motion, and a sense of entering an era instead of a page.

## Copy guardrails used in implementation

- Use sourced facts plus editorial framing.
- Avoid exact disputed performance counts when public sources conflict.
- Do not invent awards, discography, quotes, or venue claims beyond the public
  sources above.
